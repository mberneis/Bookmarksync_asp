If you select the "Download Bookmarks" check-box you will be able to see all your links on your PDA.
However, you should be aware that if you have a large collection of links, the download time will be long.
Our recommendation is to leave this box unchecked and use only the "Add URL" functionality to add bookmarks on the go.

If you do decide to download your bookmarks you should also modify your SyncIT channel settings at the AvantGo website as follows:

  • Under "MY ACCOUNT," click on SyncIT (or click the "Edit" button and then click the SyncIT channel).
  • Increase Maximum Channel Size: the channel size should be approximately the number of your bookmarks divided by 10 (i.e., if you have about 1000 bookmarks set it to 100K)
  • Increase Link Depth: set to the maximum number of folder levels that you have in your bookmark tree (9 should be sufficient, but higher numbers are acceptable)
  • Include Images: we recommend YES
  • Follow Off-site links: NO
